What is Dungeons & Dragons?

Also known as D&D, Dungeons & Dragons is a tabletop roleplaying game set in a world filled with swords and sorcery. The only limits are your imagination, and the luck your dice gives you. You roll dice to determine if your actions succeed.

Character Creation

You don't need much to start, but you do need a character. To make the character, you will go through a lengthy process doing many calculations. This tool will reduce the proccess to only 4 simple steps of choosing your options. Here are your options explained, further down.

Race

Your race is like your species, where you came from, be that an elegant elf of the forest, or a dwarf of the mountains. You can be anything from a scary half-orc to a measly halfling (also known as a hobbit) Or simply a human. Your race determines your heritage, and based on it you get some special abilities.

Class

Your class is your specialty. Sorcerers are magically gifted individuals from birth, fighters are simply fighters. This is somewhat like your job, but sometimes involves a blade or wand. Read more about the individual classes by activating the hyperlink.

Alignment

Your alignment determines the kind of person you are. These are basic guidelines to follow when acting as your character. One of the terms in your alignment denotes your relation to "law" and "chaos", while the other your relation between "good" and "evil".

Background

This explains your origins, whether you spent your life in seclusion or a criminal, or even a spy for a secret organization. You can be anyone, and this is where your character's backstory can jump off of.
